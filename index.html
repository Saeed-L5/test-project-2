About Tic Tac Toe

How to Play

Tic Tac Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game. If all nine squares are filled without either player achieving a line of three, the game ends in a draw.

History

Tic Tac Toe dates back to ancient Egypt, where similar games were played on stone surfaces. The modern version of the game became popular in the 20th century and is now a staple of childhood games worldwide.

Fun Fact

The first computer program to play Tic Tac Toe was developed in 1952 by A.S. Douglas as part of his PhD thesis on human-computer interaction.
